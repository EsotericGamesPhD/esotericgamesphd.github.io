Typers Combat turns your keyboard into a weapon. In this fast-paced typing combat game, every word you type deals damage and every mistake costs you. Battle through multiple game modes designed to test your speed, accuracy, and endurance.

Built with a striking ASCII art aesthetic — cyan, gold, red, pink, and green characters blazing across dark backgrounds — the game delivers a uniquely retro-futuristic combat experience powered by procedural audio that reacts to your gameplay in real time.

Challenge friends asynchronously with the innovative ghost code VS system: record your combat performance, share a code, and let others battle your ghost. No lobbies, no matchmaking queues — just pure skill comparison across time zones. Or jump into live ranked or unranked matches against friends and foes.

Customize your profile with unlockable ASCII art portraits, color themes, glow effects, titles, and badges. Launched simultaneously on Steam, Epic Games Store, and Android — making it one of the only typing combat games playable on mobile.

Features

⚔

Campaign Mode

Battle through progressive waves of ASCII art enemies with increasing difficulty. Prestige system with 1.6x HP scaling for infinite replayability.

♛

Ghost Code VS

Asynchronous multiplayer — record your performance, share a code, and challenge anyone to beat your ghost. No lobbies needed.

⚡

Live Multiplayer

Real-time cross-platform, cross-play matchmaking. Climb the ranked leaderboards with ELO-based ratings, or jump into unranked endless mode with friends or randoms — PC and mobile players battle together.

∞

Survival Mode

Endless endurance challenge. How long can you last as waves intensify? Compete on global leaderboards.

◎

Daily Challenges & Leaderboards

Fresh challenges every day — Speed Blitz, Accuracy, and more. Compete on global leaderboards across every mode, track your stats, and see how you stack up against the community.

◊

ASCII Art Characters

A roster of distinctive enemies and player portraits built from ASCII characters. Raccoon, Bit Crawler, Cyber Wolf, Glitch Imp, Cat, Apex Dragon, and more.

♫

Procedural Audio

Dynamic sound design that reacts to your typing and combat performance in real time. No two sessions sound the same.

✦

Deep Customization

Unlockable portraits, color themes, glow effects, border styles, titles, and badges. Make your profile your own.

☏

Cross-Platform & Cross-Play

Available on Steam, Epic Games Store, and Android with a shared multiplayer ecosystem. PC and mobile players compete on the same leaderboards, match against each other live, and share ghost codes across all platforms.

About Esoteric Games

Esoteric Games is a solo indie developer studio focused on building unique games that don't quite exist yet. Typers Combat was born from a simple wish: a typing game that felt like a real fight — not just a speed test, but a full combat experience where your words-per-minute are your weapon. Built from scratch as a one-person project, the game blends retro ASCII art aesthetics with modern multiplayer systems across PC and mobile platforms.
