Typomon Hunter is a typing RPG where you type to weaken and capture monsters. Explore procedurally generated dungeons, encounter wild Typomon built from ASCII art, and catch them by typing fast enough to fill the capture bar before they escape.

Build a team of up to six Typomon, each with unique types, passives, and abilities. Battle wild creatures and CPU trainers in typing combat where your speed and accuracy determine your damage output. Type matchups matter: Nature, Tech, Arcane, Combat, Science, Myth, Lore, and History types create a web of strengths and weaknesses.

Fuse max-evolved Typomon together with Data Splice to create powerful dual-type chimeras. Craft words from collected letters in LexiForge to boost your team's HP and damage. Upgrade your gear at the Shop with coins earned from battle.

From the solo developer behind Typers Combat, Typomon Hunter takes the typing combat formula and wraps it in a full monster-collection RPG with exploration, progression, and team building.

Features

♘

Catch Wild Typomon

Encounter ASCII art creatures in procedurally generated dungeons. Type fast enough to capture them before they flee. Each Typomon has unique stats, types, and passive abilities.

⚔

Typing Combat

Battle wild Typomon and CPU trainers by typing sentences. Your speed and accuracy are your damage. Combo multipliers and type matchups create deep tactical decisions.

⚒

8 Type System

Nature, Tech, Arcane, Combat, Science, Myth, Lore, and History. Each type has strengths and weaknesses, creating a strategic layer on top of raw typing skill.

✶

Data Splice Fusion

Fuse two max-evolved Typomon into a dual-type chimera. Irreversible and powerful. Combine passives and type advantages to create your ultimate team member.

✎

LexiForge Word Crafting

Collect letter tiles from battles and exploration. Craft words to boost your Typomon's HP and damage. Longer words and type-matched words give bigger bonuses.

⚙

Shop & Upgrades

Spend coins on permanent upgrades: Piggy Bank, Hard Drive storage, Floppy Stash, Memory Expansion for roster capacity, Rarity Scanner for rare encounters, and more.

♔

VS CPU Trainers

Face off against CPU trainers with their own Typomon teams. Read their lineup, exploit type weaknesses, and outtype them in real-time combat.

◊

ASCII Art Creatures

A full roster of Typomon built from glowing ASCII characters. Fernox, Bytox, Gryphon, Mossbyte, Hexfly, Ironmaw, Gigavox, and many more to discover and collect.

☂

Dungeon Exploration

Explore procedurally generated floors filled with encounters, items, and secrets. Each run is different. Push deeper for rarer Typomon and tougher battles.

About Esoteric Games

Esoteric Games is a solo indie developer studio focused on building unique games that don't quite exist yet. Typomon Hunter is the studio's second title, following the launch of Typers Combat. It takes the core typing combat system and wraps it in a full monster-collection RPG with exploration, team building, evolution, and fusion. Same ASCII art soul, entirely new game.
