THE STORY

A phishing link has unleashed digital chaos. Dr. Professor -- the world's most reckless professional -- clicked a suspicious email and infected the entire system with digital monsters. Now it's up to you, a skilled typist with zero patience, to dive into the corrupted network, defeat the Hoople Organization, and type your way to the source of the infection.

═══════════════════

FEATURES

TYPING IS YOUR WEAPON
This isn't a typing minigame slapped onto a battle screen. Typing IS the combat system. Your WPM determines damage output. Typos hurt your own team. Speed kills -- literally. Build combos through flawless sentences, trigger sneak attacks with split-second typing challenges, and race the clock before the enemy strikes back.
CATCH 'EM, TRAIN 'EM, FUSE 'EM
Collect 90+ species of Typomon across 8 elemental types (Science, Tech, Lore, Myth, Arcane, Nature, History, Combat), each with unique abilities. Evolve your team through typing milestones, forge words from dropped letters to boost stats, or fuse two Typomon into a powerful dual-type hybrid with the Data Splice system.
ROGUELIKE DUNGEON RUNS
Descend through procedurally generated floors with fog of war, trapped corridors, locked doors, and hidden treasure. Face 8 elemental bosses, battle the Hoople Organization's malware, solve puzzles, and push deeper into increasingly dangerous biomes -- from Digital Archives to the Void Nexus.
DEEP META-PROGRESSION
Between runs, bank your Typomon to the Hard Drive for safekeeping. Unlock and equip 30+ accessories with party-wide passive bonuses. Complete rotating quests for gold rewards. Browse the Hoople Shop for portraits, titles, name colors, and borders.
VS BATTLE MODE
Test your typing speed against 36 CPU opponents across an 8-tier ELO ranking system -- from Unranked all the way to Apex. Bring your best team, your fastest fingers, and climb the ladder. Real-time PVP coming soon.
GLITCH MODE
Activate Glitch Modes -- roguelike mutators that twist the rules in brutal ways. Enemies with +60% HP? A roster cap of one? Each glitch cranks up the difficulty and the rewards.
